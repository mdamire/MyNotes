Rekognition:
    - Finds objects, prople, text, scenes in images and videos using ML
    - Facial analysis and facial search to do user varification, people counting
    - Celebrity recognition
    - Pathing

Trascribe:
    - Auto convert speech into text
    - Uses deep learning process called automatic speech recognition (ASR)
    - Auto removes Personally Identifiable Information PII
    - Supports Auto language detection

Polly:
    - Text to speech

Translate:
    - Natural and accurate language translation
    
Lex & Connect:
    # Lex:
        "For Auto Speech Recognition chatbots"
        - Same technology that powers Alexa
        - ASR to convert Speech to Text
        - Helps build chatbots, call center bots
    
    # Connect:
        "for contact center"
        - Receive calls, create contact flows, cloud based virtual contact center
        - Can integrate with other CRM systems

Comprehend:
    - ML to find insights and relationships in text
    - Natural language processing - NLP
    - Fully managed, serverless

    - use case: analyze customer interactions (emails) to find positive or negative experience

SageMaker:
    - fully managed service for developers/data scientists to build ML models
    - Typically difficult to do all the processes in one place + provision servers

Forecast:
    - Uses ML to deliver highly accurate forecasts
    - Reduce forecasting time from months to hours

Kendra:
    - Fully managed document search service
    - extract answers within documents

Personalize:
    - ML service to build apps with real time personalized recommendations
    - Example: Personalized product recommendations
    - Integrates into existing websites, applications, SMS, ...

Textract:
    - Auto extracts text, handwriting, and data from any scanned documents using AI and ML
    - Extract data from form, tables, files
